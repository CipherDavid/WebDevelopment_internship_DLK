Tyche Trading Corporation

Shortly About US

Tyche Trading Corporation is a registered exporter of a wide variety of items including all kinds fruits, vegetables, spices, grains, rice, pulses, beans, peas and nuts since May 2011.

We specialize in exporting coconuts and coconut related product like coconut fibre, pith (peat), rope and yarn to various countries across the world. We strive to become an established and reputed exporter of Indian spices, curry powders, blended spices, condiments and other Indian commodities all over the world. We also supply custom made T. Shirts and knitted garments

We aim to provide a one stop solution to all our clients by supplying an entire gamut of different products with a wide variety all under one roof.

Products We Export / Trade

  • Coir, Peat, Rope and Yarn
  • Fruits and Vegetables
  • Pulses, Beans, Peas and Peanuts
  • Spices, Rice
  • Dried Fruits and Nuts
  • Pickles, Pappad, Honey, Egg and other packed food products
  • Salt and Sugar
  • Coconut, Sunflower and Sesame Oil
  • Activated Carbon, Areca Leaf Products, Paper cups and Plates
  • Custom made T.Shirts, Shirts and other Garments including knit wear

Our Vision and Mission

Our mission is to develop Tyche Trading Corporation into a profitable enterprise by sourcing in demand products both domestically and internationally for sale and distribution to major wholesalers and retailers around the world and to provide fully compliant, timely products and services with international standards to the global market. Our vision is to fully utilize our knowledge, creativity and collaboration to build relationships that create sustainable growth and profit for our company, and our partners, in the ever changing global marketplace.

We strive to be the premiere import and export service provider in the world. We believe in growing together and practice the principle of fair business in all our dealings.

We strive to continuously improve our efforts to ensure that our products and services exceed all our customer expectations at all times. We intend to maintain our focus on your core business applying best of our standards.
