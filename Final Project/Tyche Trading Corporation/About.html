About the Company

Tyche Trading Corporation is a registered firm established in the year 2011. We are traders and exporters of a wide variety of items including all kinds fruits, vegetables, spices, grains, rice, pulses, beans, peas and nuts. We specialize in exporting coconuts and coconut related product like coconut fibre, pith (peat), rope and yarn to various countries across the world. We strive to be an established and reputed exporter of Indian spices, curry powders, blended spices, condiments and other Indian commodities all over the world. We also supply custom made T. Shirts and knitted garments

We are proud to be a registered member of the Coconut Development Board of India (Ministry of Agriculture, Government of India).

We aim to provide a one stop solution to all our clients by supplying an entire gamut of different products with a wide variety all under one roof.

We look forward to developing a long term partnership of trust, honesty and integrity with all our customers. We invite orders on long term contract basis and paid samples can be arranged on demand by the client.

Quality

Our aim has always been to offer our customers superior quality products. To ensure this, we procure our products from reliable and quality conscious suppliers.

We take special care in proper processing and packaging. All the different stages of procurement, processing and supply are stringently monitored by respective quality control experts. Only products that conform to all established parameters of quality are finally packaged and dispatched to our customers.

Packing and Labelling

We understand the goods sent by us are of great value to our customer. Therefore we take best care for safety of the cargo by ensuring superior packaging to protect the products from spoilage during transit and delivery. We take conscious efforts to preserve the freshness, nutritional content and taste of all our food products.

We are capable to customize our packaging to meet buyers requirements. We also offer customised labelling to the products we export.
